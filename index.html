The Foundation

A hackerspace in Christchurch, New Zealand.

Located above Pomeroy's Pub, 292 Kilmore St.

Sessions each Sunday, 12-5pm.

Friendly people

Hackers, makers, of all levels and ages. Get some help, learn some new skills, or just meet new people.

Finish your project

You know. That project. The one you've never quite finished. Here's a place to get it done.

Awesome kit

3D printers, electronics, a solder station, Arduinos, all free to play with. More coming soon.
